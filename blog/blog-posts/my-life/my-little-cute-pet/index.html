My Little Cute Pet 🐢

I have two little turtles. One is bold and cute, and the other is small and shy. I love to play with them.

Xiba（西巴）

The green one is a red-eared slider, I call him Xiba（西巴）.

I've had it for almost a year now and he's still so small even though he eats a lot!

It once had an older brother, but due to my negligence, it ran away when I was changing their water.

I hope it is still alive and well. 😖

Xifei（西非）

The grey one is a West African side-necked turtle. I call him Xifei（西非）.

I've had it for almost half a year now.

It grew so fast that it was even bigger than Siba, and would chase me whenever I put my finger in the water.

Its smile is really healing. I often sit on the balcony and watch them.
